Do you like music?


I do. I honestly don't know what I would do without my Spotify account (well, probably use YouTube instead, but that's beside the point). For me at least, not a day goes by that I'm not listening to music. It helps me focus on work, enhances fun experiences and helps me cope with all the hard stuff that life sometimes throws at you. Therefore, I wanted to share a list of my favorite songs to listen to.

Psst! You can play a part of the songs by clicking on the spotify widgets to the left!

About me


Hi there! It's me! The guy who made this list!

My name is Filip Clavin. As you might be able to tell I'm pretty passionate about music. I don't really play any instruments - I only know a little piano - although I'd like to see myself as an okay singer.

As you can see my taste in music is fairly varied, in fact, it goes far beyond the genres listed here, however it would probably be an unreasonably long list if it reflected the entirety of my music taste.

Anyhow, that's a little about me. thanks for reading!
